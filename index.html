SAMRIDDHI MITHILA ART GALLERY

Know About us

Welcome to Samridhi Mithila Kala, where tradition meets artistic innovation.
I am Shalini Tiwari, a passionate artist and dedicated teacher, specializing in the enchanting Mithila art form.
With the esteemed recognition of an artisan card holder from the Government of India's Handicrafts, Ministry of textiles, my journey in preserving and sharing the rich heritage of Mithila art has flourished.
Through Samridhi Mithila Kala, I aim to not only create beautiful art but also nurture a community where this timeless tradition thrives.
Explore our gallery to witness the vibrant tapestry of Mithila artistry and join us in celebrating this ancient yet ever-evolving craft.
"Embark on a journey with Samridhi Mithila Kala, a blossoming institution dedicated to the timeless artistry of Mithila.
I am Shalini Tiwari, an ardent artist and teacher deeply committed to nurturing this rich cultural heritage.
I proudly lead this institution through its developmental phase.
Our mission is to cultivate a thriving hub for Mithila art, where tradition intertwines with innovation.
Join us as we grow, evolve, and together, weave a vibrant tapestry of creativity, preserving the essence of Mithila's artistic legacy."

Marry Joseph
