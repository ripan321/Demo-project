SAMRIDDHI MITHILA ART GALLERY

Shalini Kumari

Shalini Kumari did Honors graduation from Lalit Narayan Mithila University Darbhanga Bihar, learned Madhubani painting from an institution in Madhubani in 2003 and became a certified artist, has done B.Ed from Nilambar Pitambar University Daltonganj Jharkhand, she worked as a teacher in a private school for 8 years. But her interest towards Mithila art always remained and in 2021 she got involved in it. Formed the organization and started teaching Mithila art, where her way of teaching was greatly appreciated by her students, which encouraged her a lot. Today, through her organization 'Samriddhi Mithila Kala', she is teaching this folk art of Bihar very closely to her students living in the country and abroad. She also received recognition from the Indian Handicrafts, Ministry of Textiles, Government of India and was recognized as a Certified Mithila Artist in September 2023.
